Introduction
This project presents a comprehensive sales and operational analysis of Blinkit, a quick-commerce and online grocery retail platform. The dashboard was developed to evaluate sales performance, customer preferences, outlet efficiency, and product trends using business intelligence and data visualization techniques.
The analysis aims to provide actionable insights that support strategic decision-making, operational optimization, and revenue growth.
Project Objectives
The main objectives of this project are to:
1.	Analyze overall sales performance across different outlet categories.
2.	Identify top-performing products and item categories.
3.	Evaluate customer preferences based on product attributes.
4.	Examine outlet performance by size, type, and location tier.
5.	Generate business insights and recommendations for improved profitability.
Industry Type
The dataset belongs to the quick commerce and e-grocery retail industry, focused on fast delivery of FMCG (Fast-Moving Consumer Goods) products.
Story of the Data
The data tells the story of how a quick-commerce grocery platform sells and distributes products across multiple outlets while tracking customer preferences, sales performance, product categories, and operational efficiency.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The dashboard tracks the following KPIs:
1.	Total Sales: $1.20M
2.	Average Sales: $141
3.	Number of Items Sold: 8,523
4.	Average Customer Rating: 3.9
Dashboard Analysis and Insights
1. Sales Performance Analysis
The company generated a total revenue of $1.20M, showing strong overall market performance. Average sales remained stable at $141, indicating balanced customer purchasing behavior.
2. Outlet Establishment Trends
Sales steadily increased between 2011 and 2018, reaching a peak of approximately $205K in 2018. After 2018, sales experienced a decline before stabilizing in later years.
Insight:
The peak in 2018 suggests a period of strong expansion and market penetration.
3. Fat Content Analysis
Low-fat products generated significantly higher sales than regular products.
Insight:
Customers demonstrate a stronger preference for healthier food options, indicating changing consumer lifestyle patterns.
4. Item Type Performance
Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods emerged as the highest-performing product categories, while Seafood and Breakfast products recorded the lowest sales.
Insight:
Daily essential products contribute more consistently to company revenue.
5. Outlet Size Performance
Medium-sized outlets generated the highest sales contribution among all outlet sizes.
Insight:
Medium-sized outlets appear to provide the best balance between operational efficiency and customer reach.
6. Outlet Location Analysis
Tier 3 outlets generated the highest revenue compared to Tier 1 and Tier 2 locations.
Insight:
Tier 3 regions present stronger market demand and growth opportunities for the company.
7. Outlet Type Analysis
Supermarket Type 1 recorded the highest sales and item volume among all outlet types.
Insight:
Supermarket Type 1 serves as the company’s strongest operational and revenue-generating outlet model.
Recommendations
Expand operations and investment in Tier 3 locations.
Increase the availability and promotion of low-fat products.
Focus marketing campaigns on top-performing product categories.
Improve visibility and promotion for underperforming products.
Strengthen customer experience to improve ratings above 4.0.
Replicate the operational strategy of Supermarket Type 1 outlets.
Use predictive analytics for demand forecasting and inventory management.
Enhance promotional and loyalty programs to increase repeat purchases.
Conclusion
The analysis reveals that customer demand is strongest in Tier 3 locations, medium-sized outlets, and healthy product categories. Supermarket Type 1 outlets contribute the highest revenue and operational efficiency. The dashboard provides valuable insights that can help Blinkit optimize inventory management, improve customer satisfaction, and increase long-term profitability.
This project demonstrates the effectiveness of business intelligence and data analytics in supporting strategic decision-making within the quick-commerce retail industry.
